Seashell Exchange Program Gets Approval From Senate


A national Seashell exchange program has been launched by the government as a part of its goal to dispose of all books.


To assist the government, It is recommended that citizens replace all of their physical books (even legal ones) with authorized electronic Seashells. Any book turned in will be exchanged with a pair of Seashells for free WITHOUT Punishment.


Government officials praise this program, citing the convenience, efficiency, and purported benefits of transitioning to digitalized reading materials. The initiative, they claim, not only ensures comfortability but also eliminates the contradictory ideas that might lurk within physical books.


The Senate's endorsement of this initiative marks a significant chapter in the government's campaign to make everything digital.

National Truth Initiative Completes First Of Many School Visits


Earlier today, the National Truth Initiative traveled to schools all around the country to share the value of accurate information. The initiative aims to persuade the younger generation to report instances of unapproved reading or controversial views to the proper authorities.


National Truth Initiative and Fireman Guests educating students.



Students were treated to meticulously curated sessions that were accompanied by vivid visual aids. Plans for subsequent visits and the integration of this curriculum into the educational curriculum have already been announced, and The National Truth Initiative plans to extend its range into international countries.

Ex-Fireman Apprehended Thanks To Mechanical Hound


Only a few hours ago, a fireman guilty of murder was put to death by the Mechanical Hound. Aided by a helicopter chase, the Mechanical Hound was able to identify the culprit and swiftly execute him.


The murderer was an ex-fireman by the name of Guy Montag. Witnesses have said that using a flamethrower, he severely burned and killed the captain of the fire department. His motives are still unknown, but it is speculated that he might’ve held some sort of a personal vendetta against the captain.

Page Purge Day Marks A New Chapter Of History


People are coming together across the country to burn illegal books together in support of Page Purge Day. The recently declared national holiday shows the people’s support and commitment to the destruction of misery and unhappiness.


In cities and towns nationwide, citizens gather, carrying literature deemed dangerous by the ruling authorities.


Public citizens volunteering to burn books.



This Holiday marks the Anniversary of the first Fireman: Benjamin Franklin, who bravely burnt literature in a step toward a more harmonious and content society. Supporters assert that eliminating these forbidden texts will safeguard against the spread of disruptive and subversive ideas, ensuring unity and stability for future generations.
